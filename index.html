Only one entry per Institution is allowed. Duration of performance- 10 min.
Time for stage/ Instruments setting is maximum 5 minutes.
Maximum number of accompanists is two.
Participants must bring their own instruments, Casio not allowed.
Instruments of western origin adopted to the India Raga system are allowed.
Item can be presented in either Hindustani or Karnatk style.
Judgment will be based on the qualities like, swara, tal, selection of raga, composition and general impression.
